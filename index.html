Frequently Asked Questions

Answers to the most common questions expats ask about finding English-speaking lawyers in Ukraine.

Yes — there are numerous law firms in Ukraine that offer English-language legal services to expats, foreign nationals and international businesses. AvökatFinder lists verified English-speaking firms across Ukraine's major cities.

Use AvökatFinder's directory to search by city, practice area or firm name. All listed firms offer English-language services. You can also use the lawyer wizard to get a personalised match in under 60 seconds.

Legal fees vary by practice area and firm size. Many English-speaking firms offer a free initial consultation. Corporate and commercial work is typically billed hourly (€150–€400/hr for international-standard firms); immigration and real estate matters often have fixed fees.

The most common legal needs for expats and foreign nationals in Ukraine are immigration and residence permits, real estate purchase, employment contracts, company formation, and family law matters. Use the practice area filter to find the right specialist.

Foreign lawyers are generally not authorised to represent clients in Ukraine's courts or draft locally binding documents. You should instruct a locally qualified lawyer who speaks English and has experience working with international clients.
