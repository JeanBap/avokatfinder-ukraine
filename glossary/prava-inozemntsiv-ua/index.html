Prava Inozemntsiv na Nadbannya Neruhomosti (Права іноземців на набуття нерухомості)

Rights of foreigners to acquire real estate in Ukraine

Ukraine permits foreign citizens and legal entities to acquire most types of real estate. However, specific restrictions apply, particularly regarding agricultural land, and war-related measures have added complexity for foreign buyers. Understanding the applicable rules before committing to a Ukrainian property purchase is essential.

Foreign citizens can purchase apartments, houses, and commercial premises in Ukraine on broadly the same terms as Ukrainian citizens. A Ukrainian taxpayer identification number (РНОКПП) is required for the transaction, which foreign buyers can obtain from the State Tax Service.

How It Works in Practice

Agricultural land purchase is restricted. Under Ukraine's land market reform (effective 2021), agricultural land can initially be purchased only by Ukrainian citizens and certain Ukrainian legal entities. Foreign citizens and companies are currently prohibited from purchasing agricultural land in Ukraine.

Foreign legal entities can purchase non-agricultural real estate in Ukraine, but the transaction may require additional corporate documentation and translation of foreign company documents, including apostille certification. Foreign buyers should engage a Ukrainian notary experienced in cross-border transactions.

Currency RulesNBU foreign exchange rules apply — tightened since 2022; check current rules

Currency regulations affect how foreign buyers pay for Ukrainian property. Payments must comply with the National Bank of Ukraine's foreign exchange rules, which have been significantly tightened since 2022. Foreign buyers should confirm the current payment rules with a Ukrainian bank and legal adviser before transferring funds.

Common Mistake: Foreign buyers sometimes transfer purchase funds to Ukraine without first confirming the current National Bank regulations on incoming property payments. The rules have changed frequently since 2022, and non-compliant transfers can be blocked or subject to penalties.
Expert Tip: Engage a Kyiv-based property lawyer before starting your search. They can confirm current restrictions, advise on the РНОКПП application process, guide you on compliant fund transfer mechanisms, and represent you at the notary appointment.

Frequently Asked Questions

Can a foreigner buy an apartment in Ukraine?

Yes. Foreign citizens can purchase apartments and other non-agricultural real estate in Ukraine. A Ukrainian taxpayer identification number (РНОКПП) is required for the transaction.

Can foreigners buy agricultural land in Ukraine?

No. Agricultural land purchase is currently restricted to Ukrainian citizens and certain Ukrainian legal entities. Foreigners are prohibited.

What currency rules apply to foreign buyers?

Foreign currency payments for Ukrainian property must comply with National Bank of Ukraine regulations, which have been significantly tightened since 2022. Always confirm the current rules before transferring funds.
