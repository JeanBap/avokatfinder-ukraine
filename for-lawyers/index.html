FAQ

Questions from Law Firms

How quickly will my featured listing go live?

Within 24 hours of payment confirmation. We'll email you to gather your firm photo, bio, and contact details.

Is my firm already listed for free?

If your firm offers English-language services in Ukraine, it’s likely already in our directory as a basic listing. A featured listing upgrades your placement, adds contact details, and puts you ahead of free entries.

Can I list across multiple countries?

Yes — if your firm operates in multiple countries, we offer multi-country pricing. Email info@avokatfinder.com for a custom quote.

What does the newsletter mention include?

A 2–3 sentence spotlight of your firm with your logo, a link to your listing, and a brief description of your specialisms. Sent to our subscribers who are actively seeking legal help in Ukraine.

What does social media management cover?

4 posts per month across LinkedIn and Instagram. Our team writes the copy, sources or creates images, and handles scheduling. You approve content before it goes live. Monthly report on reach and engagement included.

What do the 4 blog posts/month in Pro include?

Our editorial team writes four SEO-optimised articles per month targeting keywords relevant to your practice areas and location. Posts are published on your avokatfinder profile page and interlinked with the wider directory — building domain authority and organic search visibility over time.

What does SEO optimisation cover in the Pro plan?

On-page optimisation (title tags, meta descriptions, headings, internal linking) and technical checks (page speed, mobile, structured data). We also track your keyword rankings monthly and adjust content strategy accordingly.

Can I cancel anytime?

Yes. All plans are month-to-month subscriptions. Cancel before your next billing date and you won’t be charged again. No contracts, no penalties.
